Стохастический градиентный спуск¶

: $$ \mathbf{w}_{k+1} = \mathbf{w}_k - \alpha_k \nabla \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{X}, \mathbf{y}\bigr). $$ ?

  1. $\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{X}, \mathbf{y}\bigr) = \frac{1}{l}\sum_{i=1}^{l}q\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{x}_i, y_i\bigr)$
  2. ?

: $$ \mathbf{w}_{k+1} = \mathbf{w}_k - \alpha_k \nabla q\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{x}_{i_k}, y_{i_k}\bigr). $$

, $i_k$ , : $$ \mathsf{E} \nabla q\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{x}_{i_k}, y_{i_k}\bigr) = \frac{1}{l}\sum_{i=1}^{l}\nabla q\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{x}_i, y_i\bigr) = \nabla\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}, \mathbf{X}, \mathbf{y}\bigr), $$ , .

Анализ сходимости стохастического градиента¶

: $$ \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}\bigr) = \frac{1}{l}\sum_{i=1}^{l}q\bigr(\mathbf{w}, i\bigr) \to \min_{\mathbf{w}}, $$ $\mathcal{L}$ $\mathbf{w}$.

: $$ \mathbf{w}_{k+1} = \mathbf{w}_k - \alpha_k \hat{\mathbf{g}}_k, \quad \mathsf{E}\hat{\mathbf{g}}_k = \mathbf{g}_k = \nabla\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}\bigr). $$

: $$ ||\textbf{w}_{k+1} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 = ||\textbf{w}_{k}-\textbf{w}^* - \alpha_{k}\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2 = ||\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 -2\alpha_{k}\hat{\textbf{g}}_k^{\mathsf{T}}(\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*) + \alpha_{k}^2||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$

$\hat{\mathbf{g}}_k$: $$ \mathsf{E}||\textbf{w}_{k+1} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 = ||\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 -2\alpha_{k}\textbf{g}_k^{\mathsf{T}}(\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*) + \alpha_{k}^2\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$ : $$ \alpha_{k}\textbf{g}_k^{\mathsf{T}}(\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*) = \frac{1}{2}||\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 - \frac{1}{2}\mathsf{E}||\textbf{w}_{k+1} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 + \frac{\alpha_{k}^2}{2}\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$

$\mathcal{L}$ : $$ \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}_k\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr) \leq\textbf{g}_k^{\mathsf{T}}(\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*) $$

: $$ \alpha_k\left(\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}_k\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr)\right) \leq \frac{1}{2}||\textbf{w}_{k} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 - \frac{1}{2}\mathsf{E}||\textbf{w}_{k+1} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 + \frac{\alpha_{k}^2}{2}\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$

$\mathbf{w}_k$ $k=0, \ldots, K$ $K + 1$ : $$ \sum_{k=0}^{K}\alpha_k\left(\mathsf{E}\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}_k\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr)\right) \leq \frac{1}{2}||\mathbf{w}_0 - \mathbf{w}^*||^2 - \frac{1}{2}\mathsf{E}||\textbf{w}_{K+1} - \textbf{w}^*||^2 + \sum_{k=0}^{K}\frac{\alpha_{k}^2}{2}\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2 \leq \frac{1}{2}||\mathbf{w}_0 - \mathbf{w}^*||^2 + \sum_{k=0}^{K}\frac{\alpha_{k}^2}{2}\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$

$\mathcal{L}$: $$ \mathsf{E}\mathcal{L}\left(\frac{1}{\sum\alpha_k}\sum\alpha_k\mathbf{w}_k\right) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr) \leq \sum_{k=0}^{K}\alpha_k\left(\mathsf{E}\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}_k\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr)\right) \leq \frac{1}{2}||\mathbf{w}_0 - \mathbf{w}^*||^2 + \sum_{k=0}^{K}\frac{\alpha_{k}^2}{2}\mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2. $$

: $$ \hat{\mathbf{w}}_{K} = \frac{1}{\sum\alpha_k}\sum\alpha_k\mathbf{w}_k, \quad ||\textbf{w}_0 - \textbf{w}^*||^2 \leq R^2, \quad \mathsf{E}||\hat{\textbf{g}}_k||^2 \leq G^2. $$

: $$ \mathsf{E}\mathcal{L}\bigr(\hat{\mathbf{w}}_K\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr) \leq \frac{R^2+G^2\sum\alpha^2_k}{2\sum\alpha_k} $$

: $$ \mathsf{E}\mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}_K\bigr) - \mathcal{L}\bigr(\mathbf{w}^*\bigr) \leq \frac{R^2+G^2\sum\alpha^2_k}{2\sum\alpha_k} $$

: $$ \quad \sum\alpha_k = \infty, \quad \sum\alpha^2_k < \infty, \qquad \left[ \alpha_k = \frac{1}{k}\right] $$ $\approx O\left(\frac{1}{\log K}\right)$.

: $$ \quad \sum\alpha_k = \infty, \quad \frac{\sum\alpha^2_k}{\sum\alpha_k} = 0, \qquad \left[ \alpha_k = \frac{1}{\sqrt{k}}\right] $$ $\approx O\left(\frac{\log K}{\sqrt{K}}\right)$.
